WE CREATE DELICIOUS MEMORIES

Progetto Italian Restaurant Dublin was opened in 2010 by Phil Neenan. Our dishes have been developed over past generations to deliver the most authentic Italian food in Dublin. Progetto is a family run Italian restaurant located in Dublin city. We are well known in Dublin for our homemade wood fired Pizzas, Pasta & Anti Pasti; delicious desserts and a wide range of best Italian Wines. LovinDublin described Progetto as an "Italian Gem" and we were listed in the top 10 Italian restaurants in Dublin.
